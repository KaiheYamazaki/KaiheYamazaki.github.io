Kaihe YAMAZAKI （山崎　開平）

2022 Apr - present:  JSPS Postodoctoral Fellow in National Institute of Polar Research

https://orcid.org/0000-0003-3631-5365

I'm a physical oceanographer interested in Southern Ocean dynamics. My studies often stem from in-situ ocean measurements (ship/float/biologging CTD, water sampling, ADCP etc.), being coupled with remote sensing, numerical simulations, and theoretical physics. I'm keen to comprehend complex ocean phenomena (e.g., eddies and wind-driven circulation) into a reductive idea, which in turn I believe enhance our knowledge on climate variability.
