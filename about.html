BIOGRAPHY

He was born in New Jersey, US, Sep 1993. But immediately moved to Kanagawa, Japan.
When he was in elementary school, the potential for cycle race was awakened.
Because he loved mathematics and drawing picture, entered college department of architecture. However he realized human-beings are merely particles in this universe. Then he withdrew in a half year, and moved northward to Sendai city.
He entered a department of physics. In spite of that, he spent much time with mountaineering club.
Mountains taught him how fascinating the Nature is, and invited him to climate study.
