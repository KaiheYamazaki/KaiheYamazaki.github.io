ON PERIPHERY OF ICY OCEAN...

The Earth is a planet of water. Water has the power of containing heat energy, and the ocean is responsible for driving the climate system.

The Southern Ocean's picture remains unclear due to the inaccessibility and the dynamical complexity associated with the presence of ice, despite its importance for the global climate.
