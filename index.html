MOTIVATION

To understand the climate variability is the CORE of our survival strategy.


The Earth is a planet of water. Because water has remarkable heat capacity among substances on the Earth, the climate is mainly controlled by the ocean dynamics(within timescales from seasons to thousands of years).
The Southern Ocean contains substantial ambiguity, despite its great importance in the global climate, primely due to its inaccessibility and dynamical complexity, which are associated with ice. Antarctic Bottom Water (AABW) is the coldest, densest, and largest watermass in the world ocean. It is believed that formation of heavy AABW is the meridional overturning circulation and modulates planetary scale heat transport.
My commitment is to contribute to knowledge of the Antarctic ocean and the climate system.
